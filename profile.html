About Us

关于【寄·忆】不仅仅是捕捉电光火石的思维的仓库，也是对未来的寄予、对过去的思忆。对超越的喜爱陪伴着我们的过去与未来，鼓励着我们不断超越。云中白鹤寄锦书，忆起流年自超越。

Sending memories is not only a warehouse for catching the thought of flint, but also a place for the future and a memory of the past. The love of transcendence accompanies our past and future, and encourages us to transcend constantly. The white crane in the clouds sends brocade books, recalling the past years since surpassing.
